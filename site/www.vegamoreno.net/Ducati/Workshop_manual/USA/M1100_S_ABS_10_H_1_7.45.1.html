Refitting the handlebar
Position and orientate the handlebar (4) so that the punch marks (A) are equidistant from the lower clamp and aligned with the face of the clamp.
Apply the recommended grease to the threads and undersides of the heads of the screws (3).
Important
Set the upper U-bolt (1) so that arrows (B) are pointing to the tank.
 
Refit the upper clamp (1) and insert the screws (3).
Tighten the screws (3) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame torque settings), applying sequence 1-2-3-4-1-2, as indicated in the figure.
